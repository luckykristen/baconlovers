THE LAST GUY IS A LIAR.

The world's oldest person, Susannah Mushatt Jones, has revealed her secret for a long life! It’s bacon.
The 116-year-old enjoys a diet of daily bacon, eggs, and grits, and she is such a big fan of the meat, that she even had a bacon-themed cake for her latest birthday.

“Bacon makes everything better.”
